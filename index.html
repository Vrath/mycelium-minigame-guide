Step 1

First steps

Use /game shop to browse available items and /game buy to purchase one. Then equip it with /game equip. Your first 200 spores go furthest on a common weapon that matches your preferred fight style.

Step 2

Spore economy

Claim wellsprings for passive income — common ones pay 20 spores/hr, rare ones up to 50. Stack that with your daily /daily bonus and chat XP ticks. Sell items you don't need: you get partial value back.

Step 3

Build around a plan

Don't treat every item as a flat stat stick. Pick a strategy (see Archetypes) and buy toward it. A coherent loadout with three synergising items will beat three random high-stat pieces almost every time.

Step 4

Wellspring timing

Guardians protect unclaimed uncommon and rare wellsprings — fight them to claim the location. Once claimed you can lose it to any challenger. Check /wellsprings for expiry times and contest the most valuable ones before they lapse.

Step 5

Test in the Arena

Arena queues are the cleanest way to stress-test a loadout without risking wellspring control. Use /arena practice for no-stakes fights, then /arena ranked once you're confident. Check the Items tab to simulate your combined stats before queuing.
